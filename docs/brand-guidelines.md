# Brand Guidelines

## Approved tagline

**Build Meticulously, Launch Smartly**

## Approved short description

**Meticulous Tech builds AI-powered software, websites, and digital solutions for modern businesses and users.**

## Tone

- Clear
- Modern
- Practical
- Startup-minded
- Confident, not inflated

## Avoid

- Overpromising
- Unverified claims
- Buzzword-heavy copy
- Public timelines unless approved
